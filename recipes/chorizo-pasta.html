Description

A basic nameless pasta dish with chorizos and onion.

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 chorizo, diced
  • 1 half tomato, diced
  • Enough pasta (screws, butterflies or similar) for 1 person
  • 1 table spoon white wine

Steps

  1. Prepare pasta according to instructions on package
  2. Fry the onion
  3. When onion is turning soft, add chorizo and tomato
  4. Fry until the chorizo is browned.
  5. Add white wine
  6. Serve with pasta
