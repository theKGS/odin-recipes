Description

Okonomiyaki is a type of japanese pancake and the name roughly translates to "fry what you like". Many ingredients except for the basic batter components are optional.

  • 1 egg
  • 1dl okonomiyaki flour
  • 1dl water
  • 150g cabbage, shredded to 3cm long strips
  • a few strips of bacon
  • some green onion
  • okonomiyaki sauce
  • japanese style mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Mix egg, flour, water and stir
  2. Add cabbage and green onion
  3. Fry the batter in a frying pan until it's brown underneath
  4. Don't spread the batter too thin. Make it about 2cm thick
  5. While it's frying, add strips of bacon on top
  6. Flip it over and fry the bacon side of the pancake
  7. When done, remove from frying pan, spread okonomiyaki sauce on top and the sprinkle mayo liberally over everything
